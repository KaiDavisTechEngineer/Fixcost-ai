# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 23, 2026_

FixCost AI ("we," "us," or "the service") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, why, and how it's used.

## TL;DR

- No account required, no email needed, nothing to sign up for
- All vehicle data and repair history live in your browser's local storage on your device
- We send your repair query to Anthropic to generate the guide — no personal identifiers attached
- We don't track you across the web, sell your data, or share it with anyone

## What we collect

### Data you enter
When you generate a repair guide, you provide:
- Vehicle year, make, model, optional trim/engine
- A description of the problem (free text)
- Optionally, your state (used to estimate regional labor costs)
- Optionally, a YouTube API key (used directly from your browser — never sent to us)

### Data we generate
- Repair guides generated by the AI for your vehicle and problem
- Timestamp of when the guide was generated

### Data stored on your device
The following stay in your browser's localStorage and are never sent to us:
- Saved vehicles ("My Garage")
- Repair history
- Settings preferences (language, default state, YouTube API key)
- Form drafts (auto-saved as you type)

You can delete all locally-stored data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data, or by clearing your browser's data for this domain.

## How we use your data

### To generate repair guides
When you tap "Generate Repair Guide," your vehicle info and problem description are sent to our server, which forwards the request to the Anthropic API (Claude). Anthropic's privacy policy applies to how they handle this data — see [anthropic.com/legal/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy).

We don't attach your IP address, user identifier, or any other personally identifying information to these requests. The only thing sent is the vehicle details and problem text you typed.

### To prevent abuse
We apply rate limiting based on IP address (10 requests per minute). IPs are stored in memory only for the rate-limit window and are discarded automatically. No persistent log of your IP is kept.

### What we don't do
- No analytics tracking that identifies you personally
- No third-party advertising
- No selling data to anyone
- No emails, ever (we don't have your email)
- No cross-site tracking cookies

## Third-party services

When you use FixCost AI, your browser may make requests to:

- **Anthropic** — to generate the AI repair guide ([privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy))
- **AutoZone, RockAuto, O'Reilly, NAPA, Amazon** — when you tap a "Shop" link, your browser opens that retailer's site directly. We don't pass any personal info; we only construct a search URL with your vehicle and the part name.
- **YouTube** — when you tap a YouTube link, your browser opens YouTube directly
- **Vercel** — hosts the site and Edge Function. Standard request logs apply ([Vercel privacy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy))

## Your rights

You can:
- Delete all data on your device any time via Settings → Clear All Data
- Use the service entirely without providing a YouTube API key, state, or trim
- Disable the AI feature entirely — the template fallback works without any external calls

You have the right to know what data we have about you. The answer is: **nothing personal**. Everything you've entered is on your device, not ours.

## Children

This service is not directed at children under 13. If you're under 13, please don't use the service.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
